Get your stimulus payment faster than a check. And get a special sign-up bonus.

Learn more
Helping those who need it most. Fast.

During the ongoing pandemic, Americans need vital financial support with as little delay as possible. Mastercard has been accelerating stimulus payment for millions of people by having funds sent directly into the cardholder’s account. It’s faster than checks.

  • I WANT TO CHECK MY PAYMENT STATUS

    Check the status of your payment and you may be able to add a prepaid card to receive your payment

    • 01

      Go to IRS.gov to see if your payment is on the way.

    • 02

      If it says “Need more information,” it means your payment was returned to the IRS and you can add card account details so you don’t have to wait for a check.

    • 03

      Acquire a new Mastercard prepaid or debit card or use one that you have. Log in to your account to get your routing information.

  • I HAVE NOT RECEIVED PREVIOUS STIMULUS

    Check the status of your payment and you may be able to add a prepaid card to receive your payment

    • 01

      Get a prepaid card

      Acquire a new Mastercard prepaid or debit card or use one you already have.

    • 02

      Do your taxes

      File a tax return and enter the account number on your card.

    • 03

      Receive your funds

      Payment will be sent directly to that account for you to use as soon as it is received, wherever Mastercard is accepted.
